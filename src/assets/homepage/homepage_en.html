Welcome to the NIST Research Library and Museum

Go to my library card

The NIST Library Search provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more. It also helps you manage your research. NIST staff, you can sign in to:

  • Manage your “Library Account”
  • Save item records to your “Favorites”
  • Save searches to your “Favorites”
  • Export item records to other applications
  • Place holds on physical items
  • Renew items you have checked out

Watch this NIST Library Search video to learn the basics of using Library Search.

Additional Library Search aids are available on the left navigation menu of the NIST Library's website.
